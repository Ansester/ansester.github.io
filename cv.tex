\section*{Research Experience}

\entry{Research Assistant: Human-AI Collaboration, NYU Abu Dhabi}{Feb 2026--Present}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Co-developing an online experiment on how AI-mediated interpretation and coordination affect collective action in shared global supply chains, with Benjamin Rosche and Hanan Salam.
  \item Designed a repeated sourcing game in which five human buyers choose fair pay, auditing, information-sharing links, communication, and threshold-dependent remediation around one supplier governed by a fixed decision rule.
  \item Developed a group-level, four-arm treatment design comparing structured information with strategic interpretation AI, normative interpretation AI, and collective action AI.
  \item Specified behavioral outcomes and mechanism measures for cooperation, responsibility attribution, behavioral homogenization, coalition formation, and persistence following AI withdrawal.
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.20em}
\entry{Research Assistant: eBRAIN Lab, NYU Abu Dhabi}{Jan 2026--Present}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Co-developed \textbf{Bio-Informed LoRA}, a parameter-efficient fine-tuning method that injects residue-level biological priors (BLOSUM62, hydrophobicity, Grantham) into LoRA adapters for ESM-2 protein language models on signal peptide prediction.
  \item Showed BLOSUM62-guided LoRA matches or exceeds full fine-tuning on SignalP6 benchmarks across MCC, cleavage-site exactness, and residue-level F1, while training approximately 3.6\% of model parameters and using approximately 43\% less peak GPU memory at the 3B-parameter scale.
  \item Designed multi-seed evaluation protocols and a one-factor sensitivity screen showing that several single-seed improvements did not replicate.
  \item Manuscript under review (see Publications and Working Papers).
\end{itemize}
