About

Research approach

I am completing a B.S. in Computer Science at New York University Abu Dhabi, with study-away terms at NYU Paris and NYU New York. I work across the full empirical cycle: formulating a question, preparing data, developing a model or system, evaluating it, and communicating the result.

With Benjamin Rosche and Hanan Salam, I am developing an online experiment on how AI-mediated interpretation and coordination affect collective action. In the eBRAIN Lab, I work on parameter-efficient adaptation of ESM-2, with an emphasis on benchmark design, sensitivity analysis, and replication across runs.
