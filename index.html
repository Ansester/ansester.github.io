Ashmit Mukherjee

Undergraduate researcher in computational social science and human–AI interaction.

I study how artificial agents reshape cooperation, coordination, and norm formation in mixed human–AI systems. My current work models multi-party social dilemmas and asks when AI-mediated interventions stabilize cooperative equilibria.

My broader work spans scientific representation learning and multilingual NLP, with a methodological interest in how models learn meaningful structure from heterogeneous data.
